# Sales performance Data-Analysis-project
Project Objective
Provide a comprehensive overview of sales performance

Summarise total quantity sold, product count, and revenue across multiple years (2020–2022).

Enable quick identification of overall business growth and trends.

Analyse sales distribution by product, category, and country

Highlight top‑performing products (e.g., Graphic Design) and categories (Courses, Diploma, Award).

Reveal regional performance differences to guide market‑specific strategies.

Track year‑over‑year (YoY) sales trends

Measure annual changes in sales volume and revenue.

Detect growth patterns or declines (e.g., drop in 2022) for strategic decision‑making.

Evaluate sales contribution by location type (Hub vs Site)

Compare operational performance across different business units.

Support resource allocation and operational efficiency improvements.

Enable data‑driven decision‑making through interactive visuals

Use filters (Country, Year) to drill down into specific insights.

Empower stakeholders to explore data dynamically and identify actionable opportunities.
